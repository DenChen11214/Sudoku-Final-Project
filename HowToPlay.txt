Sudoku is a puzzle consisting of 81 squares divided into 9 blocks, each of which contain 9 squares.

Rules: Each of the nine blocks, rows, columns has to contain all the numbers 1-9 within its squares. Each number can only appear once in a row, column or box.

Some of the numbers are already given to the player and the rest of them must be figured out.

Every puzzle has just one correct solution.
